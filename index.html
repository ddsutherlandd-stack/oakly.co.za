How It Works

Four steps. Zero manual effort.

From raw enquiry to scored, actionable lead — Oakly handles everything in between so you can focus on what matters.

01

Lead Arrives

A buyer enquires via WhatsApp, a portal, or your website. Oakly is notified instantly.

02

Oakly Qualifies

Oakly sends a structured set of questions and captures the buyer’s responses automatically.

03

AI Analysis

Responses are analysed by AI. The lead is scored hot, warm, or cold and risk factors are flagged.

04

You Close the Deal

You receive a clean lead card with a recommended action: book a viewing, refer to a bond originator, or follow up later.
